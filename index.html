About me

I am Edgar Gomes de Araújo. I was born in 1979, the same year that Queen released the song Don't Stop Me Now. I grew up in São Paulo, spend 7 years in Ghent doing a super cool work on computational modeling of microstructures and now I'm in Amsterdam developing this awesome platform.

If you want to know a little bit more, you can take a look at some of the open source projects that I've been working on.
